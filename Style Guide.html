02 · Type

General Sans. One family, six weights.

Headlines run tight (-0.02em to -0.035em). Eyebrows and CTAs run wide (+0.10em to +0.16em), uppercase. The signature pattern is a lowercase Bold tagline above an UPPERCASE subhead.

09 · Voice

Plain-spoken, confident, slightly punchy.

Compliance is a serious product. Voice should feel like a trusted operator who has seen every lawsuit and built the tools to prevent the next one. You-led, never we-led.

Use
  • "You / your" — the reader is the operator.
  • Lowercase tagline + UPPERCASE subhead pair.
  • Short imperative CTAs: BOOK A FREE DEMO, LEARN MORE.
  • Specific stakes: TCPA, FCC, FTC, state AG, class actions.
  • Acronyms always uppercase: TCPA, DNC, FCC, FTC, VoIP.
Avoid
  • Emoji. Ever.
  • Exclamation points in marketing copy.
  • "Leverage", "synergize", "empower" — generic SaaS verbs.
  • Mascots, cartoons, or playful illustration.
  • "Users" — say operators, agents, members, customers.

10 · Buttons

Square, ALL CAPS, tracked.

Primary is brand red on white. Secondary is white-with-black-border that flips on hover. Dark is the inverse, used in headers and on light surfaces. All buttons are square.

11 · Inputs

Square fields, red focus.

1px gray-20 border at rest, brand red border + 3px red glow on focus. Labels are uppercase tracked-out micro-type.

Must be a 9-digit EIN.

12 · Pills & badges

Tinted, with a status dot. Square.

Status pills are tinted backgrounds + a leading colored dot. No rounded corners, no outlines unless explicitly the outline variant. Solid red is for "new" or "live" callouts.

17 · Do & Don't

A few hard rules.

Repeat patterns from this guide. Resist the temptations below.

Do
risk less.

Lowercase Bold tagline, tight tracking, sharp corners, brand red on black.

Don't
RISK LESS!

No exclamation points. No all-caps headlines (CAPS belongs to subheads/CTAs).

Do

Square, brand red, ALL CAPS, tracked.

Don't

No pill buttons. No sentence case on CTAs.

Do
Litigator

Square pills with a leading dot — even pills are sharp.

Don't
Litigator

No rounded pills. No rounded dots. We are sharp end-to-end.
